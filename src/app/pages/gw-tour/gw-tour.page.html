Despite already having maintained trails that are open to public access, most people are either unaware or unmotivated to utilize this area on their own. By hosting tours under the guidance of biologists trained in the Manchac ecosystems, we can attract more attention to these underappreciated areas and educate the local communities about their own surrounding habitat. This also provides a fundraising opportunity for eligible biology graduate students and qualified undergraduates at Southeastern Louisiana University, as well as partnering groups who already support the Greenway and will assist in its further development in the future.
